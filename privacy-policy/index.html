Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Heimish Discount ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application and website.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information: name, phone number, email, profile photo.
  • Location data: precise GPS location during active rides to match riders with drivers and provide navigation.
  • Ride information: pickup/dropoff addresses, fare, route, ride history.
  • Payment information: processed by third-party providers; we do not store full card numbers.
  • Driver verification: driver license, vehicle registration, license plate photo.
  • Device & usage data: device type, OS, app version, crash logs.

2. How we use information

  • To provide and operate the ride-sharing service.
  • To match riders with nearby drivers.
  • To process payments and calculate fares.
  • To verify driver eligibility.
  • To improve safety, prevent fraud, and comply with legal obligations.
  • To send service-related notifications.

3. Sharing of information

We share information between the rider and matched driver to complete a trip (name, photo, rating, location). We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with payment processors, mapping providers, and as required by law.

4. Data retention

We retain ride and account data for as long as your account is active and as required for legal, accounting, and safety purposes.

5. Your rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us. You may also disable location permissions in your device settings — note that this will prevent the app from functioning.

6. Children

Heimish Discount is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the app or website.

8. Contact

For questions about this policy, email privacy@heimishdiscount.com.
